A LEGACY OF SUCCESS

Charmillion Hotels and Resorts stands as a testament to a journey of success that has spanned 24 years and continues to grow. Tracing its roots back to 2010, it first emerged as Sea Group Hotels and Resorts and was lauded for its unique services and unparalleled facilities. Over a decade, the group expanded, evolving in response to the changing needs of its guests. The year 2017 marked a significant milestone, with the group rebranding to Charmillion Hotels and Resorts. Located in regions blessed with wonderful climates and abundant sunshine, the resorts have become year-round destinations loved by many.

EXPERIENCES

Charmillion Resorts offers a multitude of activities and entertainment options that cater to all ages and interests. Whether you prefer poolside relaxation, beach lounging, engaging in our morning activities, attending our nighttime events, or providing fun for your kids at our kids club, Charmillion Resorts has you covered. We make sure every moment of your stay is filled with entertainment and enjoyment.

BEACH

Stretching over 700 meters. Our beach overlooks stunning views of Tiran Island and the notable Million Hope Wreck. We also feature a private 147-meter jetty that bridges the coral area, leading to an ideal snorkeling and diving location, and the exclusive Al Rabwa area, providing a tranquil oasis for couples.

POOLS

Our pools offer both relaxation and lively fun, all against the backdrop of beautiful sea views. The outdoor adult pool serves as a versatile spot for tranquil swims and sunbathing, as well as energetic water activities such as aqua aerobics and volleyball. As you enjoy the water, you’ll have the added bonus of a stunning sea vista enhancing your experience. Meanwhile, our dedicated children’s pool provides a secure, enjoyable hub for our younger visitors to play, making it a wonderful seaside getaway for everyone in the family.

DIVING

Plunge into an underwater realm of adventure at Charmillion. Our beach leads to a perfect spot in the Red Sea, accessed via a scenic jetty. Traverse this path to delve into crystal-clear water, where you can explore a breathtaking underwater world teeming with vibrant marine life. Whether you’re a seasoned diver or a snorkeling enthusiast, this experience promises to leave you in awe of nature’s aquatic grandeur.

ENTERTAINMENT

Immerse in a world of fun and excitement at Charmillion. Our daytime activities includes stretching, aqua gyms, water polo, beach volleyball, dance lessons, foam parties, and more. Promise exhilarating outdoor enjoyment. Engage in games like billiards, tennis, or football, available throughout the day and night. As the sun sets, head to our Roman-style outdoor Amphitheater for unforgettable evenings filled with diverse shows, performances, and competitions that cater to all tastes.

KIDS CLUB

Experience excellence in childcare at our Kids Club. We prioritize safety, supervision, and fun. Each child receives personalized attention, with a range of stimulating indoor and outdoor activities to enjoy. The club is equipped with an open-air playground and a multilingual animation team to keep the fun going. The club caters to children aged 3 to 12 years. As the day fades, the fun continues with our Kids Mini Disco at the Amphitheater, starting daily at 8:30 PM.
